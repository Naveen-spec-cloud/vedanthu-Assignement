Let's set up your account

We ask your date of birth first, so we can show you the right experience and, where the law requires it, bring in a parent or guardian.

Why we ask: India's DPDP Act, 2023 requires verifiable parental consent before we process a child's personal data.

←Step 1 of 3

You're 14 — we need a parent's OK

Because you're under 18, the DPDP Act requires verifiable consent from a parent or guardian before we can create your account and use your data.

What happens next: we'll text a code to your parent's phone. Once they verify it's them, they'll choose exactly what your account can do.

Enter the code sent to your parent
